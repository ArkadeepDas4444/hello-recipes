Hyderabadi Biryani Recipe (Dum Style)

Ingredients

For Marination:

500g Chicken, 1 cup curd, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, spices (turmeric, chili powder, coriander, garam masala), 2-3 green chilies, fried onions, mint & coriander leaves, lemon juice, salt, and oil.

For Rice:

2 cups basmati rice, whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, shah jeera), salt, and water.

For Dum Cooking:

2 tbsp ghee, saffron milk, fried onions, rose & kewra water, dough for sealing.

Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken ‚Äì Mix all marination ingredients & refrigerate for 2-4 hours.
  2. Cook Rice ‚Äì Boil water with spices, cook rice until 70% done, and drain.
  3. Layering ‚Äì In a heavy pot, layer marinated chicken, then half-cooked rice. Add saffron milk, ghee, fried onions, and rose-kewra water. Repeat layers.
  4. Dum Cooking ‚Äì Seal the lid, cook on high for 5 min, then low for 30-40 min.
  5. Serve ‚Äì Gently mix and serve hot with raita & salan.
